Welcome

My name is Jake Dumford, and I'm a professional runner competing for District Track Club in the Washington D.C. area. When I'm not running, I spend a lot of my time programming. I'm passionate about web development, and I'm trying to build my career as a front-end developer. I built this site from scratch, using the Angular JavaScript framework, and I plan to use it in a couple different ways. First I want a way for anyone who’s interested (hey mom) to be able to follow my running career as I transition from college to the next level. Second, I’m going to start to build a professional portfolio of my development work. Just like my running, and programming careers, this site is a work in progress. Over the coming months and years, I hope to add blog posts, photos, race results, software projects and whatever else I can come up with to the site. To any friends, family, fans, and potential employers, I hope you enjoy the site, and I hope you join me on this journey!
